About Us

Ice Igloo Reasearch Fundamentals

A new investment paradigm.

The ice igloo fund screener aims to help muslim's buy into underlying stock&shares assets. We want to make the impact investing process easy for the new islamic investor looking for the stock.

Shariah compliant ETF used for reasearch - MSCI Islamic Index

Impact investment ETF used for reasearch -MSCI KLD 400 Social Index

We have chosen the funds as they mimic the top companies within the index. we aim to build the funds, from these two underlying principles to better promote a better society and enviroment.
